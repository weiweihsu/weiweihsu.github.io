One of my dreams is to be able to express through illustrated comics, but I think it’s going to take a while for me to be fluent enough to tell stories in that beautiful medium.

For now, I’d like to try and make collages with photos, words, and lines and shapes! As I understand myself more, I realize that I think better in a blend of media types, and I’m looking forward to making more and iterating on comic log as a way to share stories.

Hopefully, there will be a day where they become more dynamic, partially animated, bi-directionally linked, and more. If you have suggestions and/or want to riff on this, let me know!
