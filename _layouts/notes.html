I often re-remember the times when I wrote something everyday or every month. In order to reflect upon my work and life and inspired by Matt Webb's article on Weeknotes, I've decided scribble down thoughts and group them by weeks.

In the hopes of making these weeknotes useful when looking back, I'm tagging work done under an organization/collective with the symbol &. In the future, I will sort these weeknotes by projects, to trace and see how everything unfolded. <- This did not work, I think I will need to find a different way to trace how my thinking evolve.

As these are published in very raw form, please be generous when reacting to them.
